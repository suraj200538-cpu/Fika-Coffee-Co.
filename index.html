The Vibe

More Than
Just Coffee.

Started in 2019 by two coffee-obsessed Goans, Fika Coffee Co. was born out of a simple idea — Goa deserved a proper specialty cafe. A place where the coffee is taken seriously but the atmosphere stays relaxed. Come for the coffee, stay for the vibes.
